Ananya Gubbi Mohanbabu

I am a PhD student advised by Dr. Amy Pavel at the Department of Computer Science at UT Austin. My research focuses on accessibility, usability, and UI understanding of interactive systems. Specifically, I work on creating new interactions and accessible systems that improve human-AI collaboration.
I received my Master's in Human-Computer Interaction from the School of Information at UT Austin and Bachelor's in Information Science and Engineering from RV College of Engineering.
